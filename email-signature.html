How to add it in Gmail

  1. Click Copy signature above.
  2. In Gmail, open Settings (gear icon) → See all settings.
  3. On the General tab, scroll to Signature and click Create new (name it e.g. “UpperDeck”).
  4. Click into the signature box and paste (Cmd + V).
  5. Under Signature defaults, set it for new emails and replies.
  6. Scroll down and click Save Changes.

If the logo doesn't appear right after pasting, make sure the PNG is live at the URL above, then re-paste. Gmail proxies and caches images from public URLs only (it can't use local files or SVGs).
